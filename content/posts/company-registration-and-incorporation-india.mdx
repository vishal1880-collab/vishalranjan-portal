## What is company registration in India?

Company registration in India is the legal process of incorporating a business entity under the Companies Act 2013 or the LLP Act 2008. It typically takes **7–14 working days**, costs between **₹6,000 and ₹15,000** in government fees, and is handled through the MCA's SPICe+ form. The most common entity types are Private Limited Company, LLP, One Person Company (OPC), and Public Limited Company.

| Entity Type | Min. Members | Min. Capital | Best For |
|-------------|--------------|--------------|----------|

| LLP | 2 partners | No minimum | Professional services, small business |

For most startups and growth-stage businesses, a **Private Limited Company** is recommended due to limited liability, ease of raising equity, and credibility with banks and investors.

## Documents required for company registration

For a Private Limited Company, the following are required:

**For directors and shareholders:**
1. PAN card (mandatory for Indian nationals)
2. Aadhaar card
3. Passport (mandatory for foreign nationals)
4. Address proof (bank statement, electricity bill — not older than 2 months)
5. Passport-size photographs
6. Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
7. Director Identification Number (DIN) — auto-allotted via SPICe+

**For the registered office:**
1. Proof of registered office (rent agreement or sale deed)
2. NOC from owner
3. Latest utility bill (not older than 2 months)

## Company registration process and timeline

The MCA's SPICe+ form integrates all incorporation steps:

| Step | Duration |
|------|----------|
| Name reservation (Part A of SPICe+) | 1–2 days |
| DSC issuance for directors | 1 day |
| SPICe+ Part B filing (incorporation, PAN, TAN, GST, EPFO, ESIC, bank account) | 2–3 days |
| MCA review and Certificate of Incorporation | 5–7 days |
| Post-incorporation compliance (commencement of business, INC-20A) | Within 180 days |

The entire process is online. Physical presence at MCA offices is not required.

## Company registration fees in India

Government fees depend on authorized capital:

- **Up to ₹1 lakh authorized capital**: ₹6,000–₹7,000
- **₹1 lakh to ₹5 lakh**: ₹8,000–₹10,000
- **₹5 lakh to ₹10 lakh**: ₹12,000–₹15,000
- **Stamp duty**: varies by state (₹500 to ₹15,000)

## What you receive after registration

Upon successful incorporation, you receive:

1. **Certificate of Incorporation (COI)** — issued by Registrar of Companies
2. **Corporate Identification Number (CIN)** — 21-digit unique ID
3. **Permanent Account Number (PAN)** — for the company
4. **Tax Deduction Account Number (TAN)**
5. **GSTIN** (if applied for in SPICe+)
6. **EPFO and ESIC registration** (auto-generated)
7. **Bank account** (via SPICe+ partner banks)
8. Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Articles of Association (AOA)

## Post-incorporation compliance

Within the first year, a Private Limited Company must complete:

- **INC-20A** (commencement of business) — within 180 days
- **First board meeting** — within 30 days
- **Appointment of auditor** — within 30 days
- **DIR-3 KYC** — annually for all directors
- **AOC-4 and MGT-7** — annual financial and return filings
- **Income tax return** — by 31 October (audit) or 31 July (non-audit)

## Foreign nationals and NRIs registering Indian companies

A foreign national or NRI can be a director or shareholder of an Indian Private Limited Company. Requirements:

- At least **one Indian-resident director** (stayed in India ≥182 days in previous calendar year)
- Apostilled or notarized identity and address proofs
- FDI compliance under FEMA (automatic route for most sectors)
- RBI reporting via Form FC-GPR within 30 days of share allotment

## Frequently asked questions

### How long does it take to register a company in India?

7–14 working days end-to-end, with name approval taking 1–2 days and incorporation taking 5–7 days after SPICe+ filing.

### What is the minimum capital required to register a Private Limited Company?

There is no minimum paid-up capital requirement since the Companies Amendment Act 2015. Companies can be registered with as little as ₹1 paid-up capital.

### Can a single person register a company in India?

Yes, through a One Person Company (OPC). A Private Limited Company requires minimum 2 directors and 2 shareholders.

### Can foreigners register a company in India?

Yes. At least one director must be an Indian resident (stayed in India ≥182 days in the previous year). FDI rules under FEMA apply.

### What is the difference between LLP and Private Limited Company?

LLPs have lower compliance burden and are taxed at 30% flat. Private Limited Companies can raise equity, are taxed at 22–25% (with surcharge), and have higher compliance but better fundraising and credibility.

### Do I need a physical office to register a company?

You need a registered office address with proof (rent agreement, NOC, utility bill). It can be a residential address or virtual office.

### What is INC-20A?

A mandatory declaration of commencement of business, filed within 180 days of incorporation. Penalty for non-filing is ₹50,000 for the company and ₹1,000 per day for directors.
